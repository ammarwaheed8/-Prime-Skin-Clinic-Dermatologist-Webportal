Who We Are?

Meet the Prime Skin Clinic team, dedicated to personalized care for your skin’s health and radiance.

Our Mission

At Prime Skin Clinic, our mission is to provide exceptional dermatological care tailored to each patient’s unique needs. We combine cutting-edge technology, evidence-based treatments, and a compassionate approach to deliver results that enhance both your skin’s health and your confidence.

Our Team

Led by Dr. Emran, a board-certified dermatologist with more than 4 years of experience, our team of skilled professionals is committed to excellence. Our dermatologists, nurses, and support staff work collaboratively to ensure every patient receives personalized care in a warm and welcoming environment. We stay at the forefront of dermatology by regularly participating in advanced training and staying updated with the latest industry advancements.

What We Offer

From medical dermatology services, such as acne treatment, stretch-marks management, and micro-needling treatment, to cosmetic procedures like Botox, fillers, and laser treatments, we offer a comprehensive range of services to address all your skin concerns. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology to ensure safe, effective, and comfortable treatments.

Our Commitment to You

We believe that healthy skin is a reflection of overall well-being. That’s why we take the time to listen to your concerns, educate you about your options, and create customized treatment plans that align with your goals. Your trust and satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do.
